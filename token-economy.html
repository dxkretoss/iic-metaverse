A Dual-Token Ecosystem

The IIC METAVERSE ecosystem is powered by two complementary digital assets, each designed to fulfill a distinct role within the platform:

DINAAR - a gold-backed digital asset providing stability and real-world value

SAYA - a utility-driven token enabling transactions, rewards & ecosystem participation

Together, these tokens create a hybrid economic model that balances long-term value preservation with dynamic growth potential.

DINAAR – Gold-Backed Digital Asset

DINAAR is a stable digital asset backed 1:1 by physical gold reserves, designed to provide a secure and transparent alternative to traditional currencies and volatile cryptocurrencies.

Key Features

100% Gold Backed

Each DINAAR token represents real, physical gold stored in certified reserves

Price Stability

Value is anchored to global gold market rates, reducing exposure to volatility

Shariah-Compliant

Structured in accordance with Islamic finance principles

Global Accessibility

Enables seamless cross-border transactions without traditional banking limitations

Transparent & Auditable

Blockchain records allow verification of transactions and asset backing

Value Proposition

DINAAR combines the reliability of gold with the efficiency of blockchain technology - creating a trusted store of value in the digital economy.

SAYA – Utility & Growth Token

SAYA is the core utility token of the IIC METAVERSE ecosystem, enabling transactions, incentivization, and platform activity.

Key Functions:

  • Facilitates transactions across the ecosystem
  • Supports rewards and participation incentives
  • Powers smart contract interactions
  • Enables scalable economic activity within the platform

Token Model Highlights:

  • Mining-based reward structure with controlled participation
  • Designed for sustainable distribution and long-term ecosystem growth
  • Supports both users and infrastructure participants

Value Proposition

SAYA drives the operational layer of the ecosystem, ensuring liquidity, activity, and continuous engagement.

How the System Works

The IIC METAVERSE ecosystem follows a structured and compliant process that bridges traditional banking with blockchain-based assets.

[ STEP - 1 ]

Account Establishment

Users begin by opening an account with International Investment Consortium Bank

This ensures that all participants operate within a regulated and verified financial framework.

[ STEP - 2 ]

Fund Deposit

Funds are deposited into the user’s designated account through approved banking channels.

This step establishes the user’s financial position within the ecosystem and enables access to asset-backed services.

[ STEP - 3 ]

DINAAR Acquisition

Users may then purchase DINAAR, a gold backed digital asset, through the platform.

Each DINAAR unit is linked to real-world value, providing a stable and transparent digital representation of gold-backed assets.

[ STEP - 4 ]

Holding Period

DINAAR is subject to a minimum holding period of 90 days prior to redemption.

This structured holding framework supports:

Asset stability

Ecosystem sustainability

Long-term value alignment

[ STEP - 5 ]

Value Protection Mechanism

To enhance user confidence, the system incorporates a value protection framework.

Example:

If a user acquires $100 worth of DINAAR, and the market value of gold declines during the holding period, the user’s redemption value remains protected at the original acquisition level, subject to applicable terms.
